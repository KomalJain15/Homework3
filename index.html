Projects

The White Sparrow

I founded The White Sparrow to spread awareness about cervical cancer prevention, specifically focusing on promoting the HPV vaccine. Through this initiative, I raised Rs. 30,000 for the Kolkata Gynecological Oncology Trials & Translational Research Group and organized events like Pride Month Open Mic to support marginalized communities.

IMDb Movie Rating Prediction

Using Python and machine learning models, I worked on a project predicting IMDb movie ratings based on factors like genre, box office earnings, and Rotten Tomatoes scores. This project enhanced my skills in data analysis, visualization, and model optimization.

TEDxNortheasternU Impact Coordinator
